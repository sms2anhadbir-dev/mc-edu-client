📱 How to Install — iOS

1
Tap Install above. iOS will prompt you to install the app — tap Install.
2
Wait for the download to complete. The app will appear on your home screen.
3
Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management. Find the developer certificate and tap Trust.
4
Open the app from your home screen and start playing.
